About FormLingo

Breaking down language barriers in form creation and data collection

Our Mission

FormLingo empowers organizations to create forms that transcend language barriers. We believe in making data collection accessible to everyone, regardless of their native language.

How It Works

Using Google Translate API, FormLingo automatically translates your forms into multiple languages. Responses are recorded in both the original form language and the respondent's preferred language, ensuring clear communication and accurate data collection.

Key Features

  • Real-time form translation
  • Support for multiple languages
  • Dual-language response recording
  • Easy form creation and management
  • Secure data handling

Our Team

FormLingo is built by a passionate team of developers and linguists who understand the importance of breaking down language barriers in today's global community.
